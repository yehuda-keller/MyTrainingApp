Essential Ways to Track Your Fitness
Take Body Measurements

Use a flexible tape measure to track changes in body composition. Measure areas like your waist, hips, and arms to monitor progress beyond just weight.

Take Progress Photos

Take weekly or monthly photos in the same location and lighting to visually track your progress. Wear the same clothes to see changes more clearly.

Test out Your RM (Rep-Max)

Measure the maximum weight you can lift for a given exercise. Track your RM to gauge strength improvements over time.

Create a Workout Journal

Keep a workout journal to record your exercises, progress, and goals. This can help you stay motivated and organized.

Consider Your Waist-to-Hip Ratio

Calculate your waist-to-hip ratio by dividing your waist measurement by your hip measurement. This can be an indicator of overall health and fitness.
